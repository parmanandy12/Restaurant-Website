Our Story

Serving Authentic Nepali Cuisine Since 2010

Founded by Master Chef Rajendra Thapa, Himalayan Flavors brings the authentic taste of Nepal to your table. With recipes passed down through generations, we're committed to preserving the culinary heritage of the Himalayas.

Our ingredients are sourced directly from local farmers in Nepal, ensuring freshness and supporting sustainable agriculture. Each dish is prepared with care, using traditional cooking methods that have stood the test of time.

Whether you're craving the spicy flavors of Newari cuisine or the comforting warmth of a Thakali thali, our menu offers something for everyone. Come experience the taste of Nepal at Himalayan Flavors!
